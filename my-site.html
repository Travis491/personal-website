Who I am

More than a job title.

I'm Travis — a product leader at Wayfair who gets energized by the intersection of data, design, and people. By day I'm driving strategies that move the needle on how millions of people shop online.

By night I'm happily outnumbered at home: a wife, two kids, one very good dog, and three cats who are entirely in charge of the household. I wouldn't have it any other way.

I believe the best products come from curiosity, experimentation, and a deep respect for the people using them. I'm always learning, always building, always looking for the next thing that makes a dent.
