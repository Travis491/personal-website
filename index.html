Who I am

More than a job title.

I'm Travis, a product leader based out of Toronto, currently building at Wayfair — energized by the intersection of data, design, and people. I spend my days shaping strategies and building experiences that help millions of people shop for their homes with more confidence.

Outside of work, I'm happily outnumbered by my wife, two kids, one very good dog, and three cats who seem to run the place. I wouldn't have it any other way.
